About Maze

The core concept of the film "Maze" was finalized over a week's time and I decided to portray the entire concept without any dialogues or background story telling. It was shot over a single day using just an iPhone and it was shot across multiple locations in the suburbs of Kolkata city. We travelled over 100 kms on a single day reaching different locations as per our plan and wrapping our shots. Now as the film had no dialogues so I made sure that the background score should do justice to the overall story telling so I spend some time in advance, selecting the right music. Editing was completed over a week. "Maze" was done on a very limited budget and we are all happy with the look and feel we achieved in the end.
